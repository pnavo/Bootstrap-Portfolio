About Me

Born and raised in Berkeley, California. Attended private school from Kindergarten through 8th grade at The School of the Madeleine, Saint Mary's College High School from 9-12, and after attending community college in California, graduated from the University of Hawai'i at Manoa in the spring of 2013 with an Interdisciplinary Studies degree in Nutrition and Exercise.

My professional experience has been in lifeguarding throughout my academic career, and then working in fulfillment in e-commerce previous to graduation from College. I'm able to work both indepentantly and in team settings, and respond well to high-stress environments as well as deadline driven schedules.
